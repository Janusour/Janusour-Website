About Me

My diverse interests paint a vivid picture of my cultural journey, contributing to my profound cultural awareness. As an enthusiastic follower of EXO, the South Korean-Chinese boy band, their music and visuals have influenced my appreciation for the richness of diverse artistic expressions. Cats, with their playful charm, have woven a universal thread into my life, transcending cultural boundaries. My love for Asian cuisine extends beyond flavors, becoming a gateway to understanding the cultural stories embedded in each dish. Hailing from Jordan, I cherish the traditions and vibrancy of my heritage, fostering a deep connection to Jordanian culture. Since settling in Indonesia in 2011, I've immersed myself in the local customs, embracing the warmth and hospitality that characterize Indonesian culture. These diverse interests have not only shaped my personal preferences but have also fueled my journey toward becoming a more culturally aware individual, instilling in me a profound respect for the beauty and uniqueness inherent in each cultural tapestry I encounter.
